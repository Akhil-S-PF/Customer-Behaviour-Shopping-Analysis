Business Problem Statement

A leading retail company operating through both online and offline channels wants to better understand customer shopping behavior to improve sales, customer engagement, and long-term loyalty. The company has observed changing purchasing patterns across customer demographics, product categories, seasons, and sales channels but lacks a data-driven approach to identify the key factors influencing consumer decisions.

The management team wants to analyze how discounts, product reviews, payment preferences, seasonal trends, and shopping channels impact customer purchases and repeat buying behavior. By leveraging consumer shopping data, the company aims to uncover valuable insights that can optimize marketing campaigns, improve product strategies, enhance customer retention, and support smarter business decisions.

This project focuses on performing end-to-end retail consumer behavior analysis using Python, SQL, and Power BI to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights through data cleaning, analysis, visualization, and interactive dashboards.

Project Deliverables
1. Data Preparation & Modeling (Python)
Clean and preprocess raw retail data
Handle missing values, duplicates, and inconsistencies
Perform exploratory data analysis and feature engineering
Prepare structured datasets for analysis
2. Data Analysis (SQL)
Design and query structured retail datasets
Analyze customer segments, sales trends, loyalty, and purchase drivers
Extract insights on product performance, discounts, seasonal trends, and channel-wise sales
3. Dashboard Development (Power BI)

Build an interactive dashboard to visualize:

Sales and revenue trends
Customer demographics and behavior
Product category performance
Online vs. offline sales analysis
Customer loyalty and repeat purchases
4. Report & Presentation
Summarize key findings and business insights
Provide actionable recommendations to improve customer engagement and sales performance
Create a professional stakeholder presentation
5. GitHub Repository

Maintain a well-structured repository containing:

Python scripts
SQL queries
Power BI dashboard files
Project documentation and reports
